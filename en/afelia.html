Factions & loyalties

Every power wants Afelium. None want peace. You choose who you help, who you betray — and who gets the last usable energy source.

Opale Synarchy · The Theocrats

  • They consider Afelium a “sacred light”.
  • Implants are “blessed” then welded to the bones of the faithful.
  • Public executions renamed to “purifications”.
  • Armor is grown rather than forged, using living crystals.

Heliar Wanderers · The Nomads

  • Drifters, smugglers, deserters, freelance pilots.
  • Recycle military gear and black market mods.
  • Flexible allegiance: to the highest bidder... or the fairest one.
  • Their unofficial motto: “Freedom is paid in blood.”

[ REDACTED ] · Classified File

  • AI-generated militia, bodies that never "officially" existed.
  • Total absence of biological ID in scans.
  • Non-human ballistic spread, impossible trajectories.
  • Sighted only during "blackout" events.

Combat Pillars

  • Aggression is safety — air-control, dash, wall bursts, grappling hooks on debris: if you stop, you're already dead.
  • Implant Violence — overcharge your nervous system to slow time, rip armor off targets, or project shockwaves at point-blank range.
  • Map-changing decisions — who receives the Afelium decides which districts stabilize... or collapse.
  • Betrayable coop — execute contracts with allies, then sell them out to a rival faction halfway through the operation.

Lore Briefing · Condensed

The Arcanis, Afelium, the Great Fall, the Black Monolith... and your place as the Chosen One.

It is said that several millennia ago, an extremely advanced civilization dominated the galaxy: the Arcanis. They discovered a living, conscious energy: Afelium. Thanks to it, they erected floating cities, immortal machines, and stargates connecting entire worlds.

Obsessed with absolute control, they tried to force Afelium to obey. The energy reacted. This was the Great Fall: pulverized planets, collapsed continents, cracked cities. The Arcanis almost disappeared, leaving behind only ruins and fragments of Afelium, still saturated with uncontrollable power.

Centuries later, unified humanity rediscovers a crystal vibrant with blue light: the first fragment of Afelium found. The ruins open, machines reactivate. But the energy only responds to certain rare individuals capable of synchronizing with it: the Chosen Ones.

Around them are born three visions of the future: the Opale Synarchy, which wants to rationalize and sanctify the light; the Chorus of Ashes, which sees Afelium as a living spirit to be worshipped, even if the world must burn to be reborn; the Heliar Wanderers, who reject empires and cults, and protect civilians as long as there is something left to save.

At the center of everything: Ophalen, a dead planet, and the Black Monolith, an indestructible Arcanis artifact that pulses like a sleeping heart. Some explorers claim it whispers, shows visions... and that behind the Afelium, a consciousness is awakening.

As a Chosen One, touching a fragment projects you into memories of a dead civilization. You see collapsed cities, hear voices, perceive Echoes of ancient spirits. The further you advance, the more you understand that the Arcanis may not have died: they transferred themselves into the Afelium itself.

Some of these Echoes want to be reborn by using the Chosen Ones as vessels. The Black Monolith no longer sleeps. With every mission, you decide if you save the universe from a second Great Fall... or if you repeat exactly the same mistake as them.
